Adding an image or screenshot

You can use images or screenshots from different sources and of one of these formats (GIF, JPEG, PNG) though CursorMe© will work best with PNGs not wider than your screen is.
Add images from your device (pc, mac, phone ...) by tapping the grey background of CursorMe© or the Green - Picture Button.
You can add images as well by dragging an Image-File from your computer onte the grey background of CursorMe©.
If you want to add images or screens from a website or external URL, provide a link and submit this file with clicking the Green - Link Button.

This tool uses Google Analytics Tracking. Details?

User Tracking - to improve this tool.

Tracking - Why?

This little app is pretty young and tracking anonymous usage patterns help me to find what users need, more than a survey or asking my collegues can do. While you use the Tool you help me to make it better.

What is tracked, and what isn't?

The base for tracking is google analytics. I took measures to reduce the amount of data and the level of detail that i track to a minimum. I make use of anonymize-ip setting to just get a rough idea from which region users accessing this tool, to prepare localization. I track via "events" what features of the tool are mostly used.

If you feel that this is too much information tracked, you can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking for this website.
